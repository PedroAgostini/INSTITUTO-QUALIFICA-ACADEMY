How do I get access?

As soon as your payment is confirmed, you get instant access to the Circle community for your plan, with all courses, materials, live sessions, and benefits unlocked.

You have a 7-day guarantee. The subscription is monthly, with no lock-in and no penalty, so you can cancel whenever it stops making sense for you.

Yes. The Silver and Gold plans include digital certification when you complete courses and tracks.

Is the content updated?

Constantly. The content keeps pace with the evolution of industrial and energy technologies, and new courses are added automatically for active subscribers.

You can start from fundamentals and progress to advanced technologies. The platform works on phone, tablet, and desktop, including the Circle app.
